About RestoRant the Website

A little coding project. A restaurant rant website.

Designed and developed by Noel Cendana of Temasek Polytechnic.


Walkthrough

Welcome to RestoRant! This section takes you on a walkthrough of the app and each page's functions.

Timeline

The Timeline shows all your posted reviews, favorite restaurants and your profile info, all in one easy-to-use page.

Restaurant

The Restaurant page displays information about each restaurant, as well as external links to order/reserve, get directions or learn more.

Account / Settings

Customize your account in the Account section of this page.

Discover / Search

Search, sort and filter restaurants not just on their name, but also on cuisine, dining option, establishment type or rating.

Reviews & Statistics

Every restaurant has its own statistics section which shows the average rating, total number of reviews, and number of reviews per rating.

Learn More

Find out more about the restaurants, call, reserve or book a table, get directions and more.
