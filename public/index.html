About RestoRant

RestoRant is simply a restaurant review website coded by me. It does what its name says–it’s a web-app designed for users to compliment (rave) or complain (rant) about restaurants that they have visited, and to express openly their positions and opinions about what they enjoyed or disliked in those restaurants. More restaurants will be added soon, but only if you wait!
